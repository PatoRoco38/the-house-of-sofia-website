Privacy Policy

Privacy is part of governance.

The House of Sofia® treats privacy, discretion and informational responsibility as structural elements of its institutional conduct.

1. Purpose

This Privacy Policy explains how The House of Sofia® may collect, use, store and protect information provided through its institutional website and official contact channels.

2. Information We May Collect

We may collect identification and contact information voluntarily provided by visitors, including name, email address, professional role, organization, country and message content.

3. Use of Information

Information is used exclusively for institutional communication, evaluation of inquiries, concession-related analysis, membership-related review and internal governance purposes.

4. Confidentiality

Information submitted to The House of Sofia® is treated with discretion and is not sold, publicly disclosed or shared for unrelated commercial purposes.

5. Data Storage

Information may be stored for as long as necessary to evaluate institutional requests, maintain internal records and comply with applicable legal or operational obligations.

6. User Rights

Visitors may request access, correction or deletion of personal information by contacting the official institutional communication channel.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact: contact@thehouseofsofia.com
